关于我们

湖南长沙雅绣集湘绣有限公司是湖南湘绣行业的知名品牌，是湖南省工艺美术协会常务理事单位，拥有自己的生产、加工、培训和销售团队，吸收了湘绣高级工艺师余健女士为技术总监，著名画家石君先生为设计总监，同时拥有高级工艺美术师担纲主创工艺。

2013

About us

Hunan Ya Xiu Ji Embroidery Co., Ltd. is a well-known brand in Hunan Embroidery industry, enjoy the reputation as one of the Hunan arts and crafts association executive director unit .It has it’s own production, processing ,training and marketing team, With Miss Yu Jian, the advanced technician, as it’s technical director, and famous painter Mr. Shi Jun as design director, At the same time many other senior artists has contributed to it’s creation and techniques.

2013

我们的团队

公司秉承传统与现代相结合的创新理念，推陈出新，打造出一系列观赏性与艺术性并存的原创湘绣精品，极具收藏价值。目前产品畅销全国各地，先后在美国纽约、新加坡、台湾等地售出并展示，深受业内外人士的一致好评。 公司将全方位致力于湘绣的研发与生产，为弘扬湘绣产业文化而努力。让湘绣不失于为湖南人的骄傲，成为国家的精品，迈向世界的舞台！

2013

our team

Adhering to innovative concept of combining tradition and modernity, the company has created a series of ornamental and artistic embroidery with originality and fine quality, which is valuable for collection. Currently, it selling products all over the country, and be praised by people in and outside the industry when displaying in New York, Singapore, Taiwan and other places’ show. The company has committed to the development and production of a full range of embroidery to promote embroidery industry culture. Ya Xiu Ji’s principle is to become the pride of Hunan province and boutique of China ,and move forward towards the world stage.
